Mayhaemsters is a competitive multiplayer game for up to four players where players control hamsters on paper airplanes as they fly around on a continuously morphing beach while trying to shoot each other down with the goal of being the last hamster alive.

Meanwhile, randomly appearing items can be used to influence the controls of your enemy players.

For instance, one of the more striking items cause the four major player abilities – namely Attack, Dash, Use Item and Self-Destruct - to be remapped to different action buttons over and over again.

With that, it is not possible to fully rely on your own controls in the same way as provided by typical video game conventions.

Mayhaemsters is for players looking for a fun, dynamic and unique action experience that can be shared with your friends and families.
